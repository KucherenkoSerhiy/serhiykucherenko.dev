Three directions, one site

Agreed strategy: fast minimal foundation, one or two signature interactive moments, a dual-entrance landing (clients one door, engineers the other), a lab with embedded demos plus an index of deployed apps, giscus comments on articles.

Each board renders its direction live: hover, move the cursor, drag things. The copy is real. The signature moment on each board is the actual proposed one, not a placeholder.

What stays constant in all three

Static Astro output, Lighthouse ≥95, system or self-hosted fonts only, both color themes in production (boards commit to one world each for clarity), no tracking, content readable in one click. The signature moment degrades gracefully: with JS off or prefers-reduced-motion, every page is still a complete, fast document.
