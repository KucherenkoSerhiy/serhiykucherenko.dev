Rationale

Nobody else's portfolio looks like a payment rulebook, which is exactly the point: it's his material. The dry document form sets up the joke, the red annotations deliver it. Comments-as-margin-notes turns giscus from a bolted-on widget into the identity. In production the condensed display face is self-hosted (e.g. an open grotesque condensed) rather than Arial Narrow.
